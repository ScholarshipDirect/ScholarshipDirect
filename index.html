How to Check Status Online?

Follow These Detailed Steps:

  1. Identify Your Portal: First, select your home state from the list above to go to the specific scholarship portal.
  2. Login Process: Click on the 'Student Login' button. You will need your Registration ID and Password.
  3. Verification: Solve the captcha security code carefully. If you forgot your password, use the 'Forgot Password' link.
  4. Dashboard: Once logged in, look for the 'Application Status' or 'Check Current Status' link in the menu.
  5. Print Report: It is recommended to take a screenshot or print your status for future reference in college.

Important FAQs (Help Desk)

Q1: Why is my status showing 'Pending at District Committee'?

Ans: This is a normal stage where the District Social Welfare Department verifies your documents. It usually takes 15-30 days.

Q2: My Bank status shows 'Rejected by Bank', what to do?

Ans: Immediately visit your bank branch and ask them to complete your KYC and link your Aadhaar with the NPCI mapper for DBT payments.

Q3: Can I apply for two scholarships at once?

Ans: No, as per government rules, a student can avail only one scholarship (either State or Central) in one academic year.
