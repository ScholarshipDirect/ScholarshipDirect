✅ How to Check Scholarship Status Online?

Follow these 5 steps (with screenshots guidance)

  1. Select your state portal from the grid above or go to NSP 2.0 portal.
  2. Login using Application ID / Registration number and password/DOB.
  3. Navigate to “Application Status” tab in student dashboard.
  4. Verify status: “Pending at Institute”, “Sanctioned”, “Disbursed” or “Rejected”.
  5. Track PFMS payment: Copy your bank account number and check on pfms.nic.in → Know Your Payment.

If status shows "Rejected – Bank details mismatch", visit your bank branch and update Aadhaar seeding immediately.
