UP Scholarship Status 2025-2026: A Complete Guide

Track your application and payment status online with ease.

How to Check Status?

  1. Visit the official UP Scholarship Portal.
  2. Navigate to the "Status" or "Student Login" section.
  3. Enter your Registration Number and Date of Birth.
  4. Fill in the Captcha and click on the Submit button.
  5. Your current verification status will appear on the screen.

Key Documents Needed

  • Aadhaar Card (Linked with Mobile)
  • Income & Caste Certificate
  • Previous Year Marksheet
  • Bank Passbook (NPCI Seeded)
  • Fee Receipt & Bonafide Certificate
Pro Tip: Ensure your Bank Account is mapped with NPCI. If not, your payment might get stuck even after verification.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. When will the UP Scholarship 2026 payment be disbursed?

The scholarship amount is usually transferred to students' bank accounts between February and March 2026 via DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer).

2. What does "Pending at District Scholarship Committee" mean?

It means your application has been verified at the college level and is currently awaiting final approval from the District Social Welfare Department.

3. Can I edit my form after submission?

Yes, you can edit your form only during the "Correction Window" period announced by the department. Keep checking this portal for the dates.

4. Why is my status showing 'Rejected by Bank'?

This happens if your Aadhaar is not linked to your bank account or if your KYC is incomplete. Visit your bank immediately to resolve this.

5. How to track scholarship payment via PFMS?

Go to the PFMS portal, enter your Bank Name and Account Number to track the real-time status of your scholarship credit.
