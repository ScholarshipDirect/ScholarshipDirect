Required Documents for Scholarship 2025-26

Applying for a scholarship requires careful documentation. Missing even a single paper can lead to application rejection. Below is the comprehensive state-wise document checklist for all major Indian scholarships including UP, Bihar, NSP, and more.

1. Universal Documents (Common for All States)

Before looking at specific state requirements, ensure you have these "Master Documents" ready in scanned format (PDF/JPG, usually under 200KB):

  • Aadhaar Card: Must be linked with an active mobile number.
  • Passport Size Photo: Latest color photograph with a white background.
  • Previous Marksheets: 10th, 12th, or Graduation marksheets (Original Scan).
  • Income Certificate: Must be issued by Tehsildar/Revenue Officer after April 2025.
  • Caste Certificate: Required for OBC, SC, ST, and EWS categories.
  • Domicile Certificate: Proof that you are a permanent resident of your state.

3. Bihar Post-Matric Scholarship (PMSP) Checklist

Bihar students applying through the PMSP portal need these additional documents:

  • Bonafide Certificate: Issued by the institution in the official format.
  • Fee Structure: A detailed breakdown of the institutional fee signed by the Principal.
  • Residential Proof: Bihar permanent resident certificate (Niwas Praman Patra).
  • Active Bank Account: Should be in the student's name (Joint accounts not allowed).

5. Technical Specifications for Scanning

To avoid "File Upload Failed" errors, follow these size limits:

  • Passport Photo: 20KB to 50KB (JPG).
  • Marksheets: 100KB to 200KB (PDF/JPG).
  • Certificates (Income/Caste): 50KB to 150KB (PDF).
  • Signature: 10KB to 20KB (JPG).

6. Crucial Note on Bank Aadhaar Seeding (NPCI)

⚠️ IMPORTANT: Even if your application is approved, the payment will fail if your bank account is not mapped with **NPCI (Aadhaar Seeding)**.

How to check: Visit your bank or check the "Aadhaar Seeding Status" on the UIDAI website. If it is inactive, submit a 'DBT Consent Form' to your bank manager immediately.

7. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) - Documents

Q: Can I use an old Income Certificate?
Ans: Most states require an Income Certificate issued within the last 6 months to 1 year. For the 2025-26 session, it should ideally be issued after April 1, 2025.

Q: Is it mandatory to have a bank account in a Nationalized Bank?
Ans: While not always mandatory, it is highly recommended to use accounts in banks like SBI, PNB, or BOB for faster DBT transfers.
