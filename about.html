🙏 About ScholarshipDirect

ScholarshipDirect is an independent, non-governmental information portal dedicated to helping students discover accurate scholarship opportunities. We know how many talented students miss out due to lack of timely, reliable information. Our platform is built on three pillars: transparency, zero cost, and trust.

🎯 Our Mission: “No deserving student should abandon education due to financial constraints.” We simplify scholarships, fight fraud, and guide you to official sources. We are not just a link directory — we are a student empowerment movement.

🔐 Our Commitment (AdSense Ready & Compliant)

ScholarshipDirect follows all Google AdSense policies. We never charge students any fee, we do not use misleading ads, and all content is original, helpful, and accurate. We are not affiliated with any government body, but we serve as a reliable bridge to official portals.

⚠️ Beware of Fake Scholarships

No genuine scholarship asks for money. If anyone asks for "processing fee", "security deposit", or "gift card", report immediately. ScholarshipDirect will never ask for your Aadhaar, bank OTP, or sensitive personal data. Stay vigilant.
