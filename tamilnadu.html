2. Required Documents (Checklist)

  • ✅ Community Certificate (Digitally Signed)
  • ✅ Income Certificate (Latest)
  • ✅ Nativity/Resident Certificate
  • ✅ Aadhaar Card linked with Bank (NPCI)
  • ✅ Attendance Certificate from College
  • ✅ Previous Year Marksheets

3. How to Register Online

Step 1: Obtain the application form from your Educational Institution.

Step 2: Submit all documents to the Scholarship Wing of your College.

Step 3: The Institution will upload your details to the TN State Portal.

Step 4: Once verified by the District Officer, the fund is sent via DBT to your account.
