üí° How to check UP Scholarship Status 2025-26?

Login to the scholarship portal with your ID, then click on 'Status' to see the current progress (e.g., Pending at DWO or Verified).

üí° What is the last date to apply?

The dates vary by category (9th, 10th, or Degree). Generally, applications remain open until December or January. Check the official timeline for updates.

üí° Why is my status showing "Rejected by PFMS"?

This usually means your Aadhaar is not seeded with your bank. Visit your bank branch and request to activate 'DBT' (Direct Benefit Transfer).

üí° Can I apply for two scholarships at once?

No, a student can only receive one scholarship at a time. If caught applying for two, both applications might be canceled.
