2. Document Verification (Mandatory)

Before applying on the Odisha SSP portal, ensure you have clear scanned copies of these documents:

  • Aadhaar Card: Must be linked with Mobile.
  • Resident Certificate: Issued by Tahasildar.
  • Income Certificate: From competent authority.
  • Caste Certificate: For reserved categories.
  • Copy of Passbook: For DBT payment.
  • Marksheet: Previous qualifying examination.
  • College ID: Or Admission Receipt.
  • Photograph: Passport size (Digital).

3. Step-by-Step Registration Guide

Step 1: Registration via Aadhaar
Visit the official portal and enter your Aadhaar Number. Verify using the OTP sent to your registered mobile.
Step 2: Profile Dashboard
Complete your profile by adding Academic, Personal, and Bank details.
Step 3: Select Scheme
The portal will automatically show you the schemes you are eligible for. Click 'Apply'.
Step 4: Final Submission
Review your data and click 'Final Submit'. Take a printout for your college records.
