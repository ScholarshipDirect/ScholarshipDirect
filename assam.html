Smart Application Process 2026

Step 3: Fill Institute verification through your college.

Assam Scholarship FAQ
What is the last date for Assam scholarship 2026?

NSP portal usually closes by 31st December 2026. However, DHE Assam specific schemes may have different deadlines. Always check the official portal.

Can I apply for both Post-Matric and Ishan Uday?

No, one student can claim only one government scholarship per academic year. Duplicate applications lead to rejection.

How to check Assam scholarship status 2026?

Log in to NSP portal → Application Status, or visit PFMS portal with your bank account number.
