2. Essential Documents Checklist

Ensure you have these scanned documents ready before applying:

  • Domicile Certificate of Maharashtra
  • Income Certificate (Tehsildar Signed)
  • Caste Certificate & Validity
  • Last Year Marksheet
  • Aadhaar Linked Bank Passbook
  • College Admission Receipt
  • Ration Card (Photocopy)
  • Declaration Certificate

3. How to Register on MahaDBT

1
New Registration: Use your Aadhaar Number to register. Complete the Biometric or OTP authentication.
2
Profile Completion: Fill in personal, address, and past qualification details carefully.
3
Upload Documents: Upload all original scanned documents in PDF or JPEG format.
4
Search Schemes: Based on your profile, the system will suggest eligible scholarships. Apply for the best one.

4. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: My application is under 'Scrutiny', what does it mean?

A: It means the department is currently verifying your documents. Check back in 7-10 days.

Q: Can I apply for two scholarships at once?

A: No, per government rules, a student can avail only one scholarship benefit at a time.
