2. Essential Documents for Application

Ensure these documents are scanned in JPEG/PDF format (Max 200KB):

  • Caste Certificate: Issued by the Competent Authority.
  • Income Certificate: Valid as per the current financial year.
  • Domicile Certificate: Proof of residence in Gujarat.
  • Bank Passbook: Must show Account Number and IFSC clearly.
  • Hostel Certificate: If applying as a hosteller.
  • Previous Year Marksheet: For merit verification.

3. Step-by-Step Registration

Step 1: Visit Digital Gujarat and click on "New Registration (Citizen)".

Step 2: Verify your Mobile Number and Email using OTP.

Step 3: Update your Profile with Aadhaar and Bank Seeding details.

Step 4: Select the relevant Service/Scholarship and fill the form.

Step 5: Upload documents and submit for Institute verification.
