2. Documentation & Eligibility

Before initiating the application, please verify your details against these requirements:

• White Ration Card / Rice Card (Income proof)

• Aadhaar Card (Must be e-KYC verified)

• Previous Academic Marksheets

• Valid Integrated Caste Certificate

• Bank Account Seeding with Aadhaar (NPCI)

• Admission Number provided by the College

• Passport size digital photo

• Active Mobile Number for OTPs

3. Application Submission Process

Step 1: Apply at the college/institution during admission. The college clerk will enter your details on the portal.

Step 2: You will receive a Jnanabhumi Application ID via SMS.

Step 3: Visit the portal, login with your ID, and complete the Student Verification.

Step 4: Biometric Authentication (E-KYC) is required at the nearest Village/Ward Secretariat.

Step 5: After Departmental approval, the amount is credited to the mother's bank account.
