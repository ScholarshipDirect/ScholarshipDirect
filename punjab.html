2. How to Apply (Step-by-Step)

  • Portal Registration: Visit the official website and create a unique Student ID.
  • Upload Documents: Ensure all scanned copies are under the required file size.
  • Institution Verification: Submit your application printout to your college/school.
  • Departmental Approval: Wait for the district officer to verify and approve.
  • DBT Payment: Check your bank account for the scholarship amount via Direct Benefit Transfer.

3. Documents Required (Checklist)

  • Aadhaar Card (Mandatory)
  • Punjab Resident Certificate
  • Income Certificate (Latest)
  • Caste Certificate (SC/BC/OBC)
  • Bank Passbook (Aadhaar Seeded)
  • Previous Class Marksheet
  • Fee Receipt of Current Course
  • Valid Mobile Number & Email

4. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Who is eligible for the Punjab Post-Matric Scholarship?

A: Students belonging to SC/BC/OBC categories with a family income below the specified limit (usually ₹2.5 Lakh per annum).

Q: How can I check my application status?

A: Login to the Dr. Ambedkar portal using your ID and click on the 'Track Application' tab.

Q: What is NPCI mapping?

A: It means your Aadhaar must be linked with your bank account to receive the scholarship money directly.
