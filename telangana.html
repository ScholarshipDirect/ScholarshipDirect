2. Documentation Requirements

Keep these documents ready before starting your registration to avoid rejection:

  • Aadhaar Card: Mandatory for all applicants.
  • Caste Certificate: Issued by Meeseva (with application number).
  • Income Certificate: Issued after 1st April of the current year.
  • Bank Passbook: First page scanned (Aadhaar Seeded account).
  • Previous Marksheet: Passed marksheet of the qualifying exam.
  • Bonafide Certificate: From your current College/School.

3. Step-by-Step Application Guide

Step 1: Register on the TS ePASS portal using your SSC Hall Ticket Number and Aadhaar.

Step 2: Fill in the Application Form with Personal, Academic, and Income details.

Step 3: Upload the required documents in the specified format (JPEG/PDF).

Step 4: Submit the application and take a printout of the 'Acknowledgment'.

Step 5: Submit the physical copy along with documents to your College Principal.
